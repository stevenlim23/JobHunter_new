About Us

Job Search Application Based on the World's No.1 Website

Job-Hunter is a website specifically designed to place job vacancies and for those who want to work are also able to apply.

Our Vision

We are one of the leading employer information providers in the World. We act as facilitators of job matching and communication between job seekers and employers.

Our Mission

We are committed to continuously increasing the value we provide to job seekers and employers. To fulfill this, we continue to develop our products and services so that matching between job seekers and companies becomes easier.

Growth Scheme

Founded in Indonesia in 2019, JobHunter.com currently has several reliable and talented teams, and has grown so strong in every place where we operate.

Protect Your Data Online

We are committed to protecting the privacy of your online data. As part of this commitment, our online security team always carries out regular checks on Job-Hunter.com to detect and remove suspicious job postings or outsiders.

With the available resources, as well as various filtering features available, we are always working on steps that make your job search more secure and secure.

We routinely check advertisements for illegal job openings that often ask for your personal information or financial data. Nevertheless, such illegal advertisements may still appear on Job-Hunter.com from time to time.
